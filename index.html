Common Questions

FAQ

Quick answers to things people often ask about our pharmacy.

Do you accept walk-in customers? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Walk-ins are always welcome during our opening hours (closes 9:45 PM daily). No appointment needed for standard purchases and consultations.
How do I send a prescription? ▼
You can use the form on this page to submit your prescription via WhatsApp, or simply call us on +234 806 821 5920 and we'll guide you through the process.
Are the free checkups really free? ▼
Yes! We offer complimentary health checkups for elderly individuals and children as part of our community health commitment. No hidden charges.
Do you deliver medications? ▼
Yes, we offer delivery within Oshodi-Isolo and surrounding areas. WhatsApp us at +234 806 821 5920 with your order and address to arrange delivery.
Are your vaccines properly stored? ▼
Absolutely. Vaccine storage is something we take extremely seriously. All vaccines are maintained at the correct temperatures in certified storage equipment and administered by trained staff.
Can I refill my prescription over the phone? ▼
Yes, for repeat prescriptions just give us a call or WhatsApp and we'll have your order ready for pickup or arrange delivery.
