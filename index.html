Our Story

Small but Mighty

Cozy space for 2–5 people, amazing service & atmosphere.

Hot Crust Cafe is a hidden gem inside Alpha Mall, Ogudu. Though the space is intimate (indoor ~20 guests, outdoor ~10), the food, customer service, and atmosphere are absolutely top-notch. Our chef is known for recommending the best dishes — from stir-fried pasta to Chinese rice and crispy waffles.

Whether you’re here for a quick bite, a date, or chilling with friends, Hot Crust Cafe delivers 10/10 vibes. Air conditioning on blast keeps it fresh, and our famous Oreo milkshake keeps you coming back.
